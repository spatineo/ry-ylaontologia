Tässä ontologiassa ei keskitytä revisioimaan itse suunnitelmia. Kts. "PROV does not specify such a notion of ancestor. Indeed, PROV does not deﬁne derivation as a transitive relation, nor does PROV deﬁne properties for all potential ancestors: for instance, there is no property to relate an activity to the plan an agent used, or to relate an activity to the starter activity that initiated it, despite the fact that the plan and the latter activity may be seen as ancestors." (Moreau & Groth 2013)
